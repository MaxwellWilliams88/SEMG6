Code of Conduct 

This code applies to all members of the group and must be followed completely. 

1. Group members treat each other with respect at all times
2. Bullying and harassment of any kind for any reason is not acceptable
3. Group members should do their best to support each other's learning and development
4. Group members must communicate effectively and politely to ensure that the project can be completed
5. Any disputes between individual members should be resolved, if the individuals cannot sort it the issue should be brought up to the rest of the group to help find a solution
6. Group members must do their best when contributing towards the project
7. Group members must communicate if they are having difficulty with any aspect of their assigned work
